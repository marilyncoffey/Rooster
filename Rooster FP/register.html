Registration Rules

1. Must be 13 years or older to register and use Rooster. (Or have the ability operate commercial grade farming equipment.)
2. Must have valid e-mail address.
3. Must agree to weekly Farming related emails- "The Morning Roost". (Not able to opt out.)
4. Roosting is only available from 7:00 PM to 4:00 AM. If you have to ask why you are not a farmer and are not to register.
5. Thou shall not covet thy neighbors crop or equipment.
6. This is not a dating website. To find your farming companion, please visit our partner Farmers Only.
7. This is not a sales community, to sell goods or equipment please register for our sister sales site, The Barn.
